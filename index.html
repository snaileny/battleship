Battleship Game

Drag and drop the ships to the gameboard to place them. Click on the placed ship to rotate it.

Your Turn!

  1. Easy: Computer always hits random squares.
  2. Medium: If the computer exposes a ship, it tries to continue hitting it.
  3. Hard: If the computer exposes a ship, it never misses.
  1. Game starts with your turn.
  2. If you miss (gray square), computer plays it's turn.
  3. If you hit a ship, the square you hit gets exposed (orange square) and you get an another turn.
  4. If you sink a ship, all of the ship gets exposed (red squares) and you get an another turn.
  5. You have to sink all of the enemy ships to win the game, if all of your ships are sunk, you lose the game.
